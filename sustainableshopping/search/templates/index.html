Our mission:
We like food. We like convenience. We like sustainability.
As our world gets bigger, supermarkets and chains make eating more convenient for us.
But did you know that the average meal in the US travels 1500 miles from farm to plate?
What if we could link you to local restaurants and food vendors?
Keeping the convenience AND reducing our carbon footprints.
Let's keep Earth green.
